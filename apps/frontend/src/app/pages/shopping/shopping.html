How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button at the top right of the screen and follow the provided instructions to enter your details.

I forgot my username. What should I do?

Click on the "Forgot Username" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a secure link to reset your password.

How do I update my profile information?

Once logged in, navigate to your "Account Settings" or "Profile" page. From there, you can edit your personal details, shipping address, and preferences.

How do I return books and items?

To return an item, go to your "Order History" and select "Request Return" next to the eligible item within 30 days of purchase. We will provide a printable return shipping label and instructions on how to pack your books securely.

How do I get free shipping?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over 50 €. Once your cart subtotal reaches this amount, the free shipping option will automatically become available and selected for you during checkout.
